What's New

In eXo Platform 3.5 Documentation, many features have been updated, based on improvements and changes of eXo Platform 3.5 applications, such as 2 new features updated in User Guide, including CLV templates management in Sites Explorer, and accessible media creation.

Much new content has been added to eXo Platform 3.5 Documentation. In particular, 2 significant chapters related to the way to upgrade eXo Platform from 3.0 to 3.5 are now available in Administrator and Developer Guides:

  • In Administrator Guide, the Migration chapter gives you the step-by-step instructions for migrating eXo Platform.
  • In Developer Guide, the Upgrade eXo Platform chapter helps you upgrade eXo Platform from 3.0 to 3.5 and particularly when you have customized your system (configuration, components and new features).

Also, most of guides are restructured, thus readers can easily follow instructions and have a better understanding of eXo products.

Navigating

Browse topics in the Contents frame () on the left. Click on a topic to have it displayed. Use the Back and Forward buttons to navigate within the history of viewed topics.

Searching

To quickly locate topics on a particular subject in the documentation, enter a query in the Search field. Use the Search frame () to display the Search view. You can narrow the scope of your search by selecting only the sections you are interested in.

Synchronizing

Clicking the Show in Table of Contents button () will select that topic in the navigation tree. The Link with Contents button () keeps the navigation tree synchronized to the current topic.
